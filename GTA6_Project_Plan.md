# GTA V vs GTA VI: Hype, Sales & Market Forecast Analysis

## Project Overview

This is an end-to-end Data Analyst portfolio project that compares
**Grand Theft Auto V (GTA V)** with **Grand Theft Auto VI (GTA VI)**
using historical data, public information, and clearly labeled
forecasted data.

> **Purpose:** Demonstrate real-world data analytics skills using
> Python, SQL, and Power BI.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Objectives

-   Compare the hype generated by GTA V and GTA VI.
-   Compare pre-booking (pre-order) performance.
-   Compare social media engagement at launch.
-   Compare development and marketing budgets.
-   Compare GTA V lifetime sales with GTA VI projected sales and
    revenue.
-   Build a global sales forecast by country.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Business Questions

1.  Did GTA VI generate more hype than GTA V before launch?
2.  Can GTA VI outperform GTA V in pre-orders?
3.  Which social media platform generated the highest engagement?
4.  How much larger is GTA VI's estimated development budget?
5.  Can GTA VI surpass GTA V's lifetime revenue?
6.  Which countries are expected to contribute the highest GTA VI sales?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Scope

## Module 1 --- Hype Analysis

**Metrics** - Trailer views - Trailer likes - Trailer comments - Google
Trends interest - News coverage - Hype Score

## Module 2 --- Pre-Booking Analysis

**Metrics** - Estimated pre-orders - Launch-day bookings - Pre-order
revenue (₹)

## Module 3 --- Social Media Analysis

Platforms: - YouTube - Reddit - X (Twitter) - Instagram - TikTok

Metrics: - Posts - Likes - Comments - Shares - Engagement Rate

## Module 4 --- Budget Analysis

**Metrics** - Development Budget (₹ Crore) - Marketing Budget (₹
Crore) - Total Budget (₹ Crore) - Development Time

## Module 5 --- Sales & Revenue Analysis

Historical: - GTA V yearly sales - GTA V lifetime revenue

Forecast: - GTA VI expected sales - GTA VI expected revenue - GTA VI
expected profit

## Module 6 --- Global Sales Forecast

Visualize: - Estimated copies sold by country - Estimated revenue by
country - Top 10 countries
